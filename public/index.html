WHO WE ARE?

El Toro Battalion, located in Irvine, California, is a proud unit of the U.S. Naval Sea Cadet Corps (NSCC). Originally established as the Trieste 2 Division, we transitioned in 2004 to honor the legacy of the men and women who served at the historic El Toro Air Station. With a rich history of excellence, we continue to provide a high-quality youth program for the Orange County community.

Our mission is to inspire discipline, pride, motivation, self-reliance, and self-respect in our cadets.
